  • The purpose of an emergency go back is to extend survival time in terms of disaster without immediate aid.
Content of the Bag üéí
  • Water & Canned Goods: Stock up on canned goods and water that can be stored for long periods of time without often replacement.
  • First Aid Kit: Make sure to have a first aid kit with the basic but essential items (bandaids, gauges, etc.)
  • Source of Light & Sound: Bring a flashlight to be able to see in the dark, and a whistle to be noticed by a search team.
  • Extra Clothes: Bring pieces of clothing to be able to change.
  • Hygiene Kit: Bring a hygiene kit to be able to keep clean from diseases.
  • List of Hotlines: Create a list of hotlines in case of avaible signal to contact.
